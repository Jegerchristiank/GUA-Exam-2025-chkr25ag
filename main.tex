\section*{Introduction}
\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{Introduction}
This is my two-week take-home reflection on SkillSync, the platform we assembled during the course. I keep the workshop voice but tie every answer to the readings so the examiner can follow without sitting through our project stand-ups.

The ten assignments now track the real product journey: we open with the value proposition and network loops, move through governance and inequality, and close on metrics, scaling, and the five-year bet. Figure~\ref{fig:intro-showcase} from the course fair poster keeps that path visible.
